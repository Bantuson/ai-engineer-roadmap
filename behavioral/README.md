## Track 1: Behavioral Interviews

### Key Concepts
- STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Leadership principles (Amazon LP, Google, etc.)
- Storytelling techniques
- Handling difficult questions

### Preparation Checklist
- [ ] 5-7 strong STAR stories prepared
- [ ] Stories cover different competencies
- [ ] Practice delivery (2-3 minutes each)
- [ ] Prepare follow-up answers

## Track 2: DSA/LeetCode

### Pattern-Based Approach

| Pattern | Key Problems | Difficulty |
|---------|--------------|------------|
| Arrays/Strings | Two Sum, Valid Anagram | Easy |
| Hashmaps | Group Anagrams, Top K | Easy-Medium |
| Two Pointers | Container Water, 3Sum | Medium |
| Sliding Window | Max Subarray, Min Window | Medium |
| Trees | Level Order, Validate BST | Easy-Medium |
| Graphs | BFS/DFS, Cycle Detection | Medium |
| DP | Coin Change, LCS | Medium-Hard |

### Target: 50+ problems
- Week 1: 15 problems (arrays, hashmaps, two pointers)
- Week 2: 20 problems (trees, graphs, linked lists)
- Week 3: 15 problems (recursion, DP, mixed)

## Track 3: AI Engineer Interviews

### Topics Covered
1. **Python Proficiency** - Data structures, functions, libraries
2. **Framework Knowledge** - LangChain, LlamaIndex, etc.
3. **Prompt Engineering** - Techniques, best practices
4. **Evals & Security** - Testing, vulnerabilities
5. **System Design** - Designing AI systems

### Common Questions
- How would you design a RAG system?
- Explain prompt injection and defenses
- Compare different agent frameworks
- How do you evaluate AI outputs?

## Track 4: AI PM Interviews

### Topics Covered
1. **Product Sense** - User problems, metrics, prioritization
2. **Technical Depth** - ML basics, AI capabilities
3. **AI-Specific Challenges** - Evaluation, ethics, deployment

### Common Questions
- How would you prioritize features for an AI product?
- How do you measure success for an AI feature?
- What are the challenges of deploying ML models?
- How do you handle AI bias and fairness?

## Interview Strategy

### Before the Interview
1. Research the company's AI products
2. Review job description requirements
3. Prepare relevant STAR stories
4. Practice coding problems
5. Review system design patterns

### During the Interview
1. **Listen carefully** - Understand what's being asked
2. **Clarify** - Ask questions before diving in
3. **Think aloud** - Share your reasoning
4. **Structure** - Use frameworks (STAR, etc.)
5. **Time management** - Don't get stuck

### After the Interview
1. Send thank you notes
2. Reflect on what went well/poorly
3. Note questions for future prep

## Study Schedule

### If You Have 3 Weeks

**Week 1: Foundation**
- Behavioral: Draft 5 STAR stories
- DSA: Arrays, strings, hashmaps (15 problems)
- AI: Review frameworks, basic concepts

**Week 2: Depth**
- Behavioral: Refine stories, practice delivery
- DSA: Trees, graphs, linked lists (20 problems)
- AI: System design, prompting

**Week 3: Polish**
- Behavioral: Mock interviews
- DSA: DP, mixed problems (15 problems)
- AI: Mock technical interviews

### Daily Practice
- 30 min: Behavioral story practice
- 90 min: 2-3 DSA problems
- 60 min: AI concepts/design

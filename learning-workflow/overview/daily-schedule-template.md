## Tips for Success

### Before Each Day
1. Review yesterday's reflection
2. Check today's specific plan in `week{N}/day{NN}.md`
3. Prepare prompt drill topics

### During Work Blocks
1. Use a timer for each block
2. No context switching during blocks
3. Document as you go

### After Each Day
1. Complete all tracker updates
2. Prepare tomorrow's materials
3. Get adequate sleep (at least 7 hours)

### Energy Management
- Morning: Highest energy → Creative work (prompts, Python)
- Afternoon: Medium energy → Structured learning (PM, DSA)
- Evening: Lower energy → Reflection, review

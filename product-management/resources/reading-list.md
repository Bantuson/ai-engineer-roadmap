# PM Reading List

A curated collection of books, newsletters, podcasts, and resources for product managers—organized by topic and skill level.

---

## Books

### Foundational (Start Here)

| Book | Author | Why Read It |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Inspired** | Marty Cagan | The product management bible. How top tech companies build products. |
| **The Lean Product Playbook** | Dan Olsen | Practical step-by-step for product-market fit. |
| **Escaping the Build Trap** | Melissa Perri | Why outcome-focused PM matters. Avoid becoming a feature factory. |
| **The Mom Test** | Rob Fitzpatrick | Essential user research skills. How to talk to customers without misleading yourself. |

### Strategy

| Book | Author | Why Read It |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Good Strategy Bad Strategy** | Richard Rumelt | What strategy actually is (and isn't). |
| **7 Powers** | Hamilton Helmer | Sustainable competitive advantage frameworks. |
| **Playing to Win** | A.G. Lafley & Roger Martin | Strategy as a series of choices. |
| **Crossing the Chasm** | Geoffrey Moore | Technology adoption lifecycle and market transitions. |
| **Blue Ocean Strategy** | Kim & Mauborgne | Creating uncontested market space. |

### User Research & Discovery

| Book | Author | Why Read It |
|------|--------|-------------|
| **Continuous Discovery Habits** | Teresa Torres | Modern discovery practices. Weekly user contact. |
| **Just Enough Research** | Erika Hall | Practical, no-nonsense research guide. |
| **Interviewing Users** | Steve Portigal | Deep dive on user interview techniques. |
| **Sprint** | Jake Knapp | Design sprints for rapid validation. |
| **Competing Against Luck** | Clayton Christensen | Jobs-to-be-Done framework deep dive. |

### Technical & Engineering

| Book | Author | Why Read It |

## Reading Plan Suggestions

### New to PM (First 3 Months)

1. **Inspired** by Marty Cagan
2. **The Mom Test** by Rob Fitzpatrick
3. **The Lean Product Playbook** by Dan Olsen
4. Subscribe to Lenny's Newsletter

### Intermediate PM (Deepen Skills)

1. **Continuous Discovery Habits** by Teresa Torres
2. **Escaping the Build Trap** by Melissa Perri
3. **Good Strategy Bad Strategy** by Richard Rumelt
4. Add Reforge membership if budget allows

### Senior/Staff PM (Strategic Impact)

1. **7 Powers** by Hamilton Helmer
2. **Staff Engineer** by Will Larson (understand engineering perspective)
3. **Competing Against Luck** by Clayton Christensen
4. Deep read Stratechery archives

### AI PM Specialization

1. **Prediction Machines** by Ajay Agrawal
2. **The Hundred-Page Machine Learning Book** by Andriy Burkov
3. Follow Anthropic, OpenAI, and Google AI blogs
4. Subscribe to AI-focused newsletters

## How to Use This List

1. **Don't read everything** — Pick based on current needs
2. **Apply immediately** — Reading without application is wasted
3. **Take notes** — Capture key insights for future reference
4. **Discuss with others** — Join a community or find a reading partner
5. **Revisit classics** — Great books reward re-reading as you grow
